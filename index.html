At the Legal Aid Society, I was a paralegal and Office Manager in the Juvenile Rights Division. And I learned that maybe pre-law wasn't the right choice in college. Each job had a great mission.
And I made them mine. But throughout it all, I felt the lure of coding...
I spend a lot of time on Codecademy and Udacity And when I can't find what I need online, I hit the books. Haiku Deck has a great mission, too. I'd love to make that mission ours.
